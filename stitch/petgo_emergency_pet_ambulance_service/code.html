Immediate First Aid Tips

Check Airway

Ensure nothing is blocking the throat. Keep neck straight to allow easier breathing.

Control Bleeding

Apply firm, steady pressure with a clean cloth. Do not lift to check if bleeding has stopped.

thermometer

Heat Stroke

Move to a cool area. Use room-temp water on fur. Do not use ice water as it causes shock.

psychology

Remain Calm

Pets mirror your emotions. Speak softly and move slowly to prevent further agitation.
